MEP OPENING - It's your new powerful tool for Autodesk Revit®!

Automatically generate openings in slabs and beams for pipes, cables, and ducts in an easy way.

Customize and optimize your Openings Modelling Process!

This plugin offers a range of customization options for openings modelling, providing full control over the process:

Choose between your main project or a linked model, both for the structural file (RVT or IFC) and for the MEP discipline (RVT) as well.

Specify the MEP discipline name for unique identification parameters in the created openings.

Set the standard clearance between piping and structure.

Support for circular and rectangular holes.

Select the desired nominal diameters for opening creation.

Establish a minimum allowed distance for the referenced beams and slabs.

Choose between different running scopes.

Support for floor plans and 3D views.

More than modeling, advanced features!

MEP Opening goes beyond modelling openings. Check out the additional features that make all the difference:

Automatic checking of project guidelines.

Easily visualize openings that don't respect the defined minimum distances.

Automatic identification of inverted beams.

Use of custom, standardized, and parameterized families.

Use of standardized symbology to distinguish different diameters of circular openings.

Identification of pre-existing openings, avoiding the insertion of duplicated instances.
